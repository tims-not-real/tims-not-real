TIM BOOKER

Ph.D. candidate at the
Complexity Science Hub, Vienna.

Originally from Perth, Australia.

I want to rewire our systems to create a humanity that is sustainable, equitable, fulfilling, and capable of managing the current global polycrisis.



I'm broadly interested in developing complex systems theory for governance, ideology, and social technology, with the intent to change how our systems work for the betterment and sustainability of all human life. Previously I was a visiting scholar at Shishu-kan, Kyoto University, where I studied the entropy of complex systems.
