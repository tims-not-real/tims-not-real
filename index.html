TIM BOOKER

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Complexity Science Hub, Vienna from Perth, Australia.

I want to rewire our systems to create a humanity that is sustainable, equitable, fulfilling, and capable of managing the current global polycrisis.

I currently work on theoretical foundations of complex systems, including the resilience of generalised network models and social group formation a physics-inspired lens.

Previously I was a visiting scholar at Shishu-kan, Kyoto University, where I studied the entropy of complex systems.
